📋 Project Overview & Problem Statement

Challenge: Traditional Feng Shui consultation requires expensive visits from professional masters, and most digital solutions lack authentic cultural understanding and personalized space analysis.

Solution: The Professional Feng Shui Consultant (專業風水顧問) combines AI intelligence with traditional Chinese Feng Shui principles to provide authentic, culturally-aware space optimization recommendations accessible to everyone.

Key Benefits

  • Cultural Authenticity: Deep understanding of traditional Feng Shui principles and Chinese cultural context
  • Personalized Analysis: Customized recommendations based on individual living/working spaces
  • Cost-Effective: Professional-quality consultation without expensive master fees
  • Accessibility: 24/7 availability for Feng Shui guidance and space optimization
  • Educational Value: Learn traditional principles while improving your environment
