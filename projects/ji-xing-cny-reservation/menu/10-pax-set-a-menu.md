# 10 Pax Set A — 团圆 Grand Reunion Set A

**Restaurant:** Ji Xing 吉星饭厅
**Tier:** Value
**Price:** RM 1,088 (≈ RM 109/pax)
**Courses:** 10
**Best for:** Multi-generation family reunion, large team dinner, value tier

---

## Menu

### 1. 富贵三文鱼捞生
**Prosperity Salmon Yee Sang (10-pax portion)**
Fresh salmon sashimi tossed with shredded radish, carrots, pickled ginger, candied lime, crackers, and house plum-sesame sauce.

### 2. 鸿运五福拼盘
**Five Blessings Combination Platter**
Roasted duck, char siu, soy sauce chicken, chilled jellyfish, and crispy spring rolls — generously portioned for 10.

### 3. 蟹肉海皇羹
**Crab Meat & Seafood Thick Soup**
Hand-shredded crab meat in a clear seafood broth with fish maw, conpoy, and bamboo shoots.

### 4. 清蒸顺壳鱼
**Steamed Soon Hock Fish (large)**
Whole live soon hock steamed Cantonese-style with ginger and scallion oil. Symbolises 年年有余.

### 5. 沙律凤梨虾球
**Pineapple Salad Prawn Balls**
Crispy battered prawns tossed in honey mayonnaise, served on a bed of pineapple and lettuce.

### 6. 香酥琵琶鸭
**Crispy Pipa Duck (whole duck)**
Whole roasted duck twice-cooked for crackling skin, served with house plum sauce.

### 7. 红烧海参鹅掌
**Braised Sea Cucumber with Goose Web**
Sea cucumber and braised goose web in abalone-mushroom sauce.

### 8. 蚝皇发菜双菇
**Braised Fat Choy with Twin Mushrooms**
Whole shiitake and Japanese black mushrooms with black moss in oyster sauce.

### 9. 扬州炒饭 *or* 干捞伊面
**Yang Chow Fried Rice** *or* **Longevity E-Fu Noodles** (choose one)

### 10. 红豆沙汤圆
**Red Bean Sweet Soup with Tang Yuan**
Sweet red bean broth with sesame-filled glutinous rice balls. Tang yuan symbolises family reunion (团圆).
