# 8 Pax Set A — 喜庆 Joyous Set A

**Restaurant:** Ji Xing 吉星饭厅
**Tier:** Value
**Price:** RM 888 (≈ RM 111/pax)
**Courses:** 9
**Best for:** Extended family or office team CNY dinner, value tier

---

## Menu

### 1. 富贵三文鱼捞生
**Prosperity Salmon Yee Sang (large portion)**
Fresh salmon sashimi tossed with shredded radish, carrots, pickled ginger, candied lime, crackers, and house plum-sesame sauce — sized for 8.

### 2. 锦绣五福拼盘
**Five Treasures Combination Platter**
Roasted duck, char siu, soy sauce chicken, chilled jellyfish, and crispy spring rolls.

### 3. 蟹肉海皇羹
**Crab Meat & Seafood Thick Soup**
Hand-shredded crab meat in a clear seafood broth with fish maw, conpoy, and bamboo shoots.

### 4. 清蒸顺壳鱼
**Steamed Soon Hock Fish (Marble Goby)**
Whole live soon hock steamed Cantonese-style with ginger and scallion. Symbolises 年年有余.

### 5. 沙律凤梨虾球
**Pineapple Salad Prawn Balls**
Crispy battered prawns tossed in honey mayonnaise, served on a bed of pineapple and lettuce.

### 6. 香酥琵琶鸭
**Crispy Pipa Duck (whole duck)**
Whole roasted duck twice-cooked for crackling skin, served with house plum sauce.

### 7. 蚝皇发菜冬菇北菇
**Braised Fat Choy with Twin Mushrooms**
Whole shiitake and Japanese black mushrooms with black moss in oyster sauce.

### 8. 扬州炒饭 *or* 干捞伊面
**Yang Chow Fried Rice** *or* **Longevity E-Fu Noodles** (choose one)

### 9. 莲子百合红豆沙
**Red Bean Sweet Soup with Lotus Seeds & Lily Bulbs**
Slow-simmered red bean dessert. Lotus seeds (莲子) symbolise abundant offspring.
