# 6 Pax Set A — 团圆 Reunion Set A

**Restaurant:** Ji Xing 吉星饭厅
**Tier:** Value
**Price:** RM 688 (≈ RM 115/pax)
**Courses:** 8
**Best for:** Family reunion dinner, casual CNY gathering

---

## Menu

### 1. 富贵三文鱼捞生
**Prosperity Salmon Yee Sang**
Fresh salmon sashimi tossed with shredded radish, carrots, pickled ginger, candied lime, crackers, and house plum-sesame sauce. The traditional CNY toss for rising fortune (步步高升).

### 2. 锦绣四宝拼盘
**Four Treasures Combination Platter**
Roasted duck (烧鸭), char siu (蜜汁叉烧), soy sauce chicken (豉油鸡), and chilled jellyfish (凉拌海蜇).

### 3. 干贝海皇羹
**Conpoy & Seafood Thick Soup**
Slow-simmered broth with dried conpoy, crab meat, fish maw strips, and bamboo shoots.

### 4. 清蒸鲈鱼
**Steamed Sea Bass with Superior Soy**
Whole sea bass steamed with ginger, scallion oil, and superior soy. Symbolises 年年有余 — abundance every year.

### 5. 沙律虾球
**Crispy Salad Prawn Balls**
Tempura-battered prawns coated in honey mayonnaise, served on shredded lettuce.

### 6. 香酥琵琶鸭
**Crispy Pipa Duck**
Half duck marinated 24 hours, twice-cooked for crackling skin, served with pickled cucumber.

### 7. 蚝皇发菜冬菇
**Braised Black Mushrooms with Fat Choy & Seasonal Greens**
Whole shiitake mushrooms and black moss (发菜) in oyster sauce. Fat Choy (发菜) sounds like 发财 — wishing prosperity.

### 8. 扬州炒饭 *or* 干捞伊面
**Yang Chow Fried Rice** *or* **Dry-Tossed Longevity E-Fu Noodles** (choose one)
E-Fu noodles symbolise long life (长寿).

### 9. 红豆沙汤圆 *(complimentary dessert)*
**Red Bean Soup with Glutinous Rice Balls**
Sweet red bean broth with sesame-filled tang yuan. Tang yuan (汤圆) symbolises family reunion (团圆).

## Auspicious Highlights
- 鱼 Fish — 年年有余 abundance every year
- 发菜 Fat Choy — 发财 wealth and prosperity
- 长寿面 Longevity Noodles — long life
- 汤圆 Tang Yuan — family reunion
