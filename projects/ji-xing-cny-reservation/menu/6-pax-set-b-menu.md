# 6 Pax Set B — 富贵 Prosperity Set B

**Restaurant:** Ji Xing 吉星饭厅
**Tier:** Premium
**Price:** RM 888 (≈ RM 148/pax)
**Courses:** 9
**Best for:** Family reunion dinner with premium ingredients, in-laws or VIP guests

---

## Menu

### 1. 鲍鱼三文鱼捞生
**Abalone & Salmon Yee Sang**
Sliced South African abalone alongside fresh salmon, tossed with shredded vegetables, candied citrus, golden crackers, and Ji Xing's signature plum-sesame dressing. Premium upgrade from the standard salmon toss.

### 2. 五福临门拼盘
**Five Blessings Combination Platter**
Roasted duck, char siu, soy sauce chicken, drunken chicken (醉鸡), and chilled jellyfish — five auspicious starters representing Five Blessings (五福).

### 3. 鲍鱼海皇羹
**Mini Abalone & Seafood Thick Soup**
Whole baby abalone served in a rich seafood broth with crab meat, fish maw strips, and dried scallop.

### 4. 清蒸笋壳鱼
**Steamed Soon Hock Fish (Marble Goby)**
Whole live soon hock steamed Cantonese-style — the gold standard for CNY banquet fish, prized for its sweet, firm flesh.

### 5. 黄金虾球
**Golden Salted Egg Prawns**
Wok-fried tiger prawns in a creamy salted egg yolk sauce with curry leaves.

### 6. 红烧海参鹅掌
**Braised Sea Cucumber with Goose Web**
Sea cucumber and braised goose web in a rich abalone-mushroom sauce. Sea cucumber (海参) symbolises a generous heart and good fortune.

### 7. 蚝皇发菜双菇
**Braised Fat Choy with Black Mushroom & Sea Moss**
Premium shiitake and Japanese black mushrooms with fat choy in oyster sauce.

### 8. 干捞伊面
**Dry-Tossed Longevity E-Fu Noodles**
With shredded XO scallop and chives. Longevity noodles symbolise 长命百岁.

### 9. 椰汁西米露
**Chilled Coconut Sago with Mango**
Refreshing pearl sago in fresh coconut milk topped with diced mango.
