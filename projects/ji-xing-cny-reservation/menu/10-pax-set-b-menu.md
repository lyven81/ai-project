## Menu

### 1. 龙虾鲍鱼捞生
**Lobster & Abalone Yee Sang** ⭐ *Signature*
Sliced fresh Boston lobster and South African abalone tossed with shredded vegetables, candied citrus, gold-leaf crackers, and Ji Xing's house plum-sesame dressing. The signature toss for the highest tier.

### 2. 鸿运六福拼盘
**Six Blessings Grand Platter**
Roasted duck, honey-glazed char siu, soy sauce chicken, drunken chicken, smoked salmon roll, and chilled jellyfish.

### 3. 鲍鱼花胶羹
**Abalone & Fish Maw Thick Soup**
Whole baby abalone and premium fish maw slow-simmered in a rich seafood broth with crab meat and conpoy.

### 4. 清蒸大老虎斑
**Steamed Large Tiger Garoupa**
Whole live large tiger garoupa steamed Cantonese-style — the premium grade prized for its sweet, firm flesh.

### 5. 芝士焗龙虾
**Cheese-Baked Boston Lobster** ⭐ *Signature centrepiece*
Whole Boston lobster baked with creamy mornay sauce and gratinated cheese. The CNY centrepiece — auspicious in colour (red) and form (the dragon shrimp 龙虾).

### 6. 红烧海参花胶
**Braised Sea Cucumber with Fish Maw**
Premium sea cucumber and fish maw braised in superior abalone-mushroom sauce.

### 7. 蜜汁烤乳猪
**Honey-Glazed Roast Suckling Pig** ⭐ *Signature*
Whole suckling pig roasted to crackling perfection, served two ways: crispy skin with mantou buns, then minced meat with lettuce wraps. A traditional symbol of peace and family unity.

### 8. 蚝皇发菜三菇
**Braised Fat Choy with Three Premium Mushrooms**
Shiitake, Japanese black mushroom, and golden mushroom with fat choy in superior oyster sauce.

### 9. 瑶柱蛋白炒饭
**Conpoy & Egg White Fried Rice**
Egg-white fried rice wok-tossed with shredded conpoy and chives — light and elegant after the rich courses.

### 10. 杏汁雪耳炖燕窝
**Double-Boiled Bird's Nest with Snow Fungus & Almond Cream** ⭐ *Signature*
Premium bird's nest slow-cooked with snow fungus and almond. The most auspicious finale dessert — bird's nest (燕窝) symbolises good health and longevity for the new year.
