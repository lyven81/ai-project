# 8 Pax Set B — 鸿福 Grand Blessing Set B

**Restaurant:** Ji Xing 吉星饭厅
**Tier:** Premium
**Price:** RM 988 (≈ RM 124/pax)
**Courses:** 9
**Best for:** Extended family CNY dinner with premium proteins, business hosting

---

## Menu

### 1. 鲍鱼三文鱼捞生
**Abalone & Salmon Yee Sang (large portion)**
Sliced South African abalone alongside fresh salmon, tossed with shredded vegetables and Ji Xing's signature plum-sesame dressing. Sized for 8.

### 2. 富贵六福拼盘
**Six Blessings Combination Platter**
Roasted duck, char siu, soy sauce chicken, drunken chicken, smoked salmon roll, and chilled jellyfish.

### 3. 鲍鱼蟹肉羹
**Abalone & Crab Meat Thick Soup**
Sliced baby abalone and hand-shredded crab meat in a rich seafood broth with fish maw and conpoy.

### 4. 清蒸老虎斑
**Steamed Tiger Garoupa**
Whole live tiger garoupa steamed Cantonese-style. A premium step up from soon hock — firmer flesh, prized at CNY banquets.

### 5. 黄金虾球
**Golden Salted Egg Prawns**
Tiger prawns in a creamy salted egg sauce with curry leaves.

### 6. 红烧海参花胶
**Braised Sea Cucumber with Fish Maw**
Sea cucumber (海参) and fish maw (花胶) braised in abalone-mushroom sauce. Both symbols of generosity and good fortune.

### 7. 蚝皇发菜双菇
**Braised Fat Choy with Twin Premium Mushrooms**
Whole shiitake and Japanese black mushrooms with fat choy in superior oyster sauce.

### 8. 瑶柱蛋白炒饭
**Conpoy & Egg White Fried Rice**
Premium upgrade from standard fried rice — egg-white wok-tossed with shredded conpoy and chives.

### 9. 杏汁雪梨炖银耳
**Double-Boiled Snow Pear with Snow Fungus & Almond Cream**
Slow-cooked dessert prized for nourishing properties. Snow fungus (银耳) symbolises silver wealth.
