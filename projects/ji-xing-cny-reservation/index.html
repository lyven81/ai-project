常见问题 Frequently Asked Questions

When are CNY 2026 reservations open? 2026 年新年订座何时开放？
Bookings open from 1 January 2026 to 31 January 2027 for sittings during 14–22 February 2026 (CNY 2026 festival window).订位时段为 2026 年 1 月 1 日至 2027 年 1 月 31 日；用餐期为 2026 年 2 月 14 日至 22 日（农历新年节庆期间）。
What is the cancellation policy? 取消政策？
Free cancellation 48 hours or more before your sitting. Cancellations under 48 hours forfeit 50% of the deposit. No-shows forfeit the full deposit.用餐前 48 小时以上免费取消；48 小时内取消扣 50% 订金；缺席则订金全数没收。
Is a deposit required at booking? 是否需要预付订金？
Yes — 50% deposit is required at booking to confirm your reservation. The balance is settled at the venue.是。预订时需支付 50% 订金以确认订位，余款于到店时结清。
Are vegetarian options available? 是否提供素食选项？
Yes, every set menu has a full vegetarian alternative. Please select "Vegetarian" in the dietary preference field at booking, and notify us of any additional dietary requirements.是。每款套餐皆备全素版本。预订时请于"饮食偏好"栏选择"全素"，并告知特别要求。
Is Ji Xing halal certified? 吉星饭厅是否清真认证？
Ji Xing is non-halal. Pork dishes (char siu, suckling pig) feature in several courses. We offer a "No-Pork" option on every set — char siu is replaced with honey-glazed chicken, and the suckling pig in 10-pax Set B is replaced with crispy roast duck.本店非清真认证，部分菜式含猪肉（叉烧、乳猪）。每款套餐均提供"无猪肉"选项：叉烧改为蜜汁烤鸡，十人套 B 的乳猪改为脆皮烧鸭。
What's the difference between Set A and Set B? A 套餐与 B 套餐有何不同？
Set A is our value tier — classic Cantonese banquet courses (sea bass, crispy duck, fried rice). Set B is the premium tier — abalone yee sang, soon hock or tiger garoupa, sea cucumber, fish maw, and bird's nest dessert. The 10-pax Set B is our signature Lucky Star tier with cheese-baked lobster centrepiece, suckling pig, and bird's nest dessert.A 套餐为实惠版——经典粤式宴菜（鲈鱼、脆皮鸭、扬州炒饭）。B 套餐为升级版——鲍鱼捞生、笋壳鱼或老虎斑、海参、花胶、燕窝甜品。十人套 B 为本店招牌"吉星套餐"，含芝士焗龙虾、烤乳猪、燕窝压轴。
Are private rooms available? 是否提供贵宾房？
Yes — we have 4 private rooms (small for 6-pax, medium for 8-pax, large for 10-pax, plus a VIP Room). Private rooms are first-come-first-serve. Surcharge: RM 100 (small), RM 150 (medium), RM 200 (large). The VIP Room is bundled exclusively with the 10-pax Set B (Lucky Star Signature) — no surcharge, included in the price.是。本店共 4 间贵宾房（6 人小房、8 人中房、10 人大房，及 VIP 房），先订先得。附加费：RM 100（小）、RM 150（中）、RM 200（大）。VIP 房专属十人套 B（吉星招牌），不另收费，含于套餐价内。
What if my party size doesn't match the set size? 人数不符合套餐规格怎么办？
Set menus are sized to the table — a 6-pax set seats 6, an 8-pax seats 8. If your group is smaller, you'll still pay for the full set. If larger, please book the next size up. For unusual sizes, call us directly at +60-82-148-888.套餐按桌人数定价。人数较少仍需支付整桌套餐价；人数较多请订更大套餐。如有特殊人数，请致电 +60-82-148-888。
Can I add lion dance, premium yee sang upgrade, or tea ceremony? 可加订醒狮、升级捞生或茶艺仪式吗？
Yes — these add-ons are arranged directly with the venue, not through this booking page. Mention them in the "Special Requests" field at booking, and our manager will contact you to confirm pricing and availability. Lion dance booking requires 7 days' advance notice.可以。此类附加服务由本店直接安排，非经此线上预订页面。预订时请于"特殊要求"栏注明，经理将联系确认价格与档期。醒狮预订需提前 7 天通知。
How do I find Ji Xing? 如何前往吉星饭厅？
No. 8, Jalan Nanas, 93400 Kuching, Sarawak. Free parking is available next to the building. Public transport: Grab is the easiest option in Kuching — fare from city centre is approximately RM 8–12.地址：古晋诗巫路 8 号，邮区 93400。建筑旁设免费停车场。建议使用 Grab，市中心起跳价约 RM 8–12。
Can I book by phone instead? 可以电话预订吗？
Yes — call us at +60-82-148-888 during business hours. However, online booking through this page is the fastest way to confirm your slot, especially for Reunion Eve and Day 1 sittings which book out quickly.可以。营业时间内致电 +60-82-148-888 即可。线上订位是最快确认席位的方式，尤其除夕与初一晚市迅速售罄。
What happens if I'm late for my sitting? 如果迟到怎么办？
We hold tables for 30 minutes past the sitting start time. If you're running late, please call us as soon as possible. After 30 minutes, the table may be released to walk-ins, and the deposit is forfeited.本店为已订座者保留座位 30 分钟。如有延误请尽早来电。逾 30 分钟未到，座位将释放给散客，订金不予退还。
Can I reschedule to a different sitting? 可以更改时段吗？
Yes — use the "Manage Booking" link in the header. You can change date, sitting, or party size up to 48 hours before the original sitting at no charge, subject to availability.可以。点击页面顶部的"管理预订"。原用餐时段前 48 小时以上可免费更改日期、时段或人数，视空位情况而定。
Do you offer takeaway or delivery for CNY menus? 新年套餐是否提供外带或外送？
During CNY 2026, our kitchen is fully committed to dine-in service. Takeaway and delivery for CNY set menus are not available. Reunion dinner is best enjoyed at the table.2026 年新年期间，厨房专注堂食服务，不设外带与外送。团圆饭仍以围桌共享最为应景。
