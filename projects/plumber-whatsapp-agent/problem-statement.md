## The Problem

Encik Rajan is a solo plumber serving PJ, Subang, and Shah Alam. His entire business runs on WhatsApp — customers text him to ask about leaks, clogs, and installations, and he replies when he can. But for most of the day, his hands are wet and his phone is in his pocket, so messages sit unread for 1-2 hours at a time.

By the time he checks, 2-3 customers have already texted another plumber. WhatsApp Business auto-reply only sends a canned "I'll get back to you" — it can't scope the problem, give a price range, or book a time slot. Full chatbot platforms like GoHighLevel cost RM200-500/month, are built for marketing agencies, and require technical setup a solo tradesman won't do. Service marketplaces like Kaodim charge per-lead commissions and strip away the direct customer relationship.

## Who It Is For

- Solo plumber or 1-2 person plumbing team in the Klang Valley
- Handles 4-6 jobs per day, earns RM8,000-15,000/month
- All customer communication happens on personal WhatsApp
- No website, no booking system, no admin assistant
- Loses 2-3 jobs per week due to delayed replies while on-site

## Market Fit Verdict

**Upgrades existing**

An AI agent that handles multi-turn WhatsApp conversations in English-Malay mix, scopes plumbing problems, quotes from a simple config, and books time slots — at near-zero cost via BYO LLM key, with no platform subscription. 4-5x more token-efficient than general-purpose agent frameworks.
