# How to Use Sundry Shop Assistant

## What This App Does

Sundry Shop Assistant lets you ask your own sales data questions out loud in Malay, and hear the answers spoken back in seconds. It's designed for busy shop owners who cannot stop serving customers to look at a dashboard. The agent is named Adam.

## How to Start

1. Open the app on your phone (tap the shortcut or the web link).
2. Tap the big green "Mula" button on the welcome screen.
3. You will see the main conversation screen with a microphone button.

## How to Ask a Question by Voice

1. Tap the microphone button — it turns green to show it is listening.
2. Speak your question in Malay, like: *"Hari ini jualan berapa?"*
3. The app shows what it heard as text, so you can verify.
4. Adam speaks the answer back in Malay and also shows it on screen.
5. Tap the microphone again for your next question, or interrupt Adam anytime to ask a follow-up.

## If the Shop is Too Noisy to Speak or Listen

You have four modes. Switch anytime — no restart needed.

- **🎙️ Cakap + 🔊 Dengar** — speak and listen (default, hands-free)
- **🎙️ Cakap + 📄 Baca** — speak your question, read the answer (noisy shop)
- **⌨️ Taip + 🔊 Dengar** — type your question, listen to the answer (customer nearby, can't speak)
- **⌨️ Taip + 📄 Baca** — type and read (fully silent, discreet)

Use the two toggles at the top of the screen to switch.

## What Kind of Questions You Can Ask

**Daily sales check**
- *"Hari ini jualan berapa?"* — How much did I sell today?
- *"Minggu ni macam mana?"* — How's this week going?
- *"Hari paling laku bulan ni?"* — Best day this month?

**Product categories**
- *"Kategori apa paling laku?"* — Which category sells best?
- *"Apa paling slow?"* — What's my slowest category?
- *"Bakery macam mana?"* — How's my bakery category doing?

**Customers**
- *"Member atau visitor spend lebih?"* — Members or walk-ins — who spends more?
- *"Member berapa peratus jualan?"* — What % of sales come from members?

**Payments**
- *"Cash atau card lebih banyak?"* — Cash or card — which is more?
- *"DuitNow naik tak?"* — Is digital payment growing?

**Basket size**
- *"Average transaksi berapa?"* — What's the average basket size?
- *"Kategori mana basket paling besar?"* — Which category has the biggest basket?

## Tips

- Speak naturally — no need to shout or speak robotically.
- If Adam mishears, just ask again or switch to ⌨️ Taip mode.
- The transcript shows every question and answer — scroll up to review.
- Short questions work best. *"Jualan hari ini?"* beats a long sentence.
- Prop the phone on your counter so you don't need to hold it.

## Saving Your Work

Every question and answer is saved automatically in your phone's browser for this session. When you close the app the session resets, which keeps your sales data private and the app fast. Nothing is sent to any cloud storage without your agreement.
