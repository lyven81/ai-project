# The Nine Year Plan (2022–2031)

The Nine Year Plan is the first major undertaking in a new twenty-five-year series of global Plans launched by the Universal House of Justice at Riḍván 2022. It follows a transformative quarter-century (1996–2021) during which the Bahá'í world developed an "undreamed-of capacity to learn, to grow, and to serve humanity."

## Overarching Aim

The release of the **society-building power of the Faith** in ever-greater measures. This single aim is pursued simultaneously at three levels:
- The **individual believer**
- The **local community**
- The **institutions** of the Administrative Order

## Duration and Context

- Duration: Riḍván 2022 → Riḍván 2031 (9 years)
- Part of a 25-year series concluding at Riḍván 2046
- First Plan of the third epoch of the Divine Plan
- Preceded by the Five Year Plan (2016–2021)

## Historic Significance

> "The Plan that will shortly commence… will make demands of the individual believer, the community, and the institutions reminiscent of the demands that the Guardian made of the Bahá'í world at the outset of the Ten Year Crusade." — UHJ, 30 December 2021
