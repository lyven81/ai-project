# Social Action & Public Discourse

## Growth Since 1996
- Sustained development projects: 250 → **1,500**
- Bahá'í-inspired organisations: 40 → **160+**
- Short-duration grassroots initiatives: **70,000+/year** (fifty-fold increase)

## Bahá'í International Development Organization (BIDO)
Established to support and coordinate social action globally. Provides dedicated stimulus and ensures learning from the grassroots is widely disseminated.

## Participation in Public Discourse
- Bahá'í International Community added Offices in Africa, Asia, and Europe
- National Offices of External Affairs greatly fortified
- Goal: offer a Bahá'í perspective grounded in the oneness of humanity — not political advocacy

## Institute for Studies in Global Prosperity (ISGP)
Offers undergraduate and graduate seminars exploring disciplines in the light of Bahá'í teachings. Now serves youth from 100+ countries.

## Houses of Worship (Mashriqu'l-Adhkár)
- Last Mother Temple erected in Santiago, Chile
- Two national and five local Mashriqu'l-Adhkárs under construction
- Houses of Worship in Battambang, Cambodia and Norte del Cauca, Colombia have opened
- Shrines of Bahá'u'lláh and the Báb: UNESCO World Heritage sites, up to 1.5 million visitors/year
