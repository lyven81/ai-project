# The Framework for Action

"A framework is not a formula." It provides a coherent structure within which communities develop patterns of action suited to their specific circumstances.

## The Cluster
The geographic unit of planning — "a manageable size with distinct social and economic features." Plans are made at the cluster level, organised into three-month cycles.

## Continuum of Development
Every cluster is on a continuum. Progress along this continuum is the measure of collective achievement. Milestones mark key points:
- **Milestone 1:** First intensive programme of growth established
- **Milestone 2:** Growth accelerating systematically
- **Milestone 3:** Community learns to welcome large numbers

By 2021: **1,000 clusters** had passed milestone 3 in nearly 100 countries.

## Three-Month Cycles
Plan → Act → Reflect → Renew plan. Allows communities to learn quickly and refine efforts without rigid long-term commitments.

## Training Institutes
329 national and regional institutes operate the Ruhi sequence. By 2021: 750,000+ people had completed at least one course; 2 million course completions overall.

## Culture of Learning
"Any effort to advance begins with an orientation towards learning." Be willing to act, make mistakes, reflect, and refine — without hesitating to act.

## Key Terms
- **Entry by troops** — Large-scale embrace of the Faith, the result of purposeful community building
- **Intensive programme of growth** — ~4,000 clusters worldwide
- **Society-building power** — The inherent power of the Faith to transform social reality
- **Homefront pioneer** — Believer who moves within their country to open new localities
