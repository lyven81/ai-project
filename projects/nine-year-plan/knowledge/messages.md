# The Ten Messages

## 1. 25 November 2020 — To the Bahá'ís of the World
Announced the Nine Year Plan to begin at Riḍván 2022, preceded by a One Year Plan. Described the community's resilience during the global health crisis.

## 2. Riḍván 2021 — To the Bahá'ís of the World ⭐
A sweeping review of 25 years (1996–2021). Called the Five Year Plan's achievements "breathtaking." Announced the third epoch of the Divine Plan. Detailed milestone-3 growth to 1,000 clusters.

## 3. 25 November 2021 — To Friends in the Holy Land
Addressed representatives at the centenary commemoration of 'Abdu'l-Bahá's Ascension. Reflected on the advancements of the period and the demands ahead.

## 4. 27 November 2021 — Tribute to 'Abdu'l-Bahá
A deeply moving tribute. Described His unique role as Centre of the Covenant, quoting Shoghi Effendi's celebrated description of Him as the "stainless Mirror" and "perfect Exemplar."

## 5. 1 December 2021 — To the Bahá'ís of the World
Brief post-gathering message. Expressed confidence that the spiritual forces generated by the centenary gathering would inspire communities for the Plan ahead.

## 6. 30 December 2021 — To the Conference of Counsellors ⭐
The most comprehensive statement of the Nine Year Plan. Introduced "society-building power" as the overarching aim. Addressed community building, social action, institutions, and the historic significance of the Plan.

## 7. 1 January 2022 — To all National Spiritual Assemblies ⭐
Addressed training institute materials. Confirmed the Ruhi sequence continues. Authorized institutes to develop supplementary materials and branch courses.

## 8. 3 January 2022 — To the Auxiliary Board Members
Addressed Auxiliary Board members' vital role at the grassroots. Called on them to help communities overcome challenges, maintain focus, and build "havens of peace."

## 9. 4 January 2022 — To the Bahá'ís of the World ⭐
Formal launch of the Nine Year Plan. Announced worldwide conferences heralding its beginning. A call to every believer to arise and serve.

## 10. Unfoldment of the Divine Plan — Chart
Visual reference tracing all Plans from 'Abdu'l-Bahá's Tablets (1916–17) to the present, showing epochs and stages in their historical sequence.
