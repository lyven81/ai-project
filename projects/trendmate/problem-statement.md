# Problem Statement — Malaysian Fashion Seller Research Assistant

## The Problem

Malaysian online fashion sellers on Shopee, Lazada, and TikTok Shop make most of their product, pricing, and restocking decisions based on gut feel. They have no dedicated analyst, no market research tool built for their scale, and no time to manually monitor competitors, trends, and platform changes every day.

The result: they miss trend windows, overprice or underprice products, sit on dead stock, and lose sales to more informed competitors.

## The Core Issues

- Trend windows in online fashion are short — sometimes just 2–3 weeks
- Competitor pricing changes daily and is hard to track manually
- Shopee/Lazada/TikTok Shop algorithm and policy updates are frequent and not widely communicated
- Seasonal events (Raya, CNY, 11.11, 12.12) require early preparation but sellers often react too late
- Sourcing decisions (local vs. China suppliers) lack data

## Who Has This Problem

- Small-to-mid online fashion sellers (1–10 person teams)
- Solo sellers managing their own Shopee/Lazada/TikTok Shop stores
- Dropshippers looking for the next trending product
- Fashion boutique owners expanding online

## What They Currently Do

They scroll TikTok and Instagram manually to spot trends. They check competitor shops one by one. They ask supplier groups in WhatsApp for what's selling. They make buying decisions based on what sold well last season.

## What We Are Building

An AI-powered market research assistant built specifically for Malaysian online fashion sellers. It searches for trending styles, monitors competitor activity, tracks platform updates, and flags upcoming seasonal opportunities — all in plain, actionable language.

## The Outcome

Sellers make faster, more confident decisions on what to stock, how to price it, and when to promote it. They catch trends early instead of late. They stop wasting money on dead stock.

## Key Areas Covered

- Trending clothing styles in Malaysia (weekly)
- Competitor pricing and product activity on Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop
- Wholesale supplier options (local and China sourcing)
- Platform policy and algorithm updates
- Malaysian seasonal sales calendar (Raya, CNY, 11.11, 12.12, school holidays)
- Fashion hashtag and keyword trends on TikTok and Instagram
