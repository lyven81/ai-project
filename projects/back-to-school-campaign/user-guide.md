# How to Use Back-to-School

## What This App Does
You type in your campaign details once. Five AI copywriters each produce copy for a different platform — WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, Blog, and SEO — all in Bahasa Melayu. You copy and paste each one into the actual platform.

## How to Start
1. Open `demo.html` in your browser.
2. Enter your Gemini API key (get one free at ai.google.dev). The key stays in your browser only — it is never sent anywhere except Google.
3. Fill in the campaign brief form on the left, or click one of the 3 preset buttons.

## How to Use
1. Fill in your store name, products, prices, campaign dates, and target audience.
2. Pick a tone: Formal BM, BM Santai, or Campur.
3. Click "Jana Kempen" (Generate Campaign).
4. Wait 5–10 seconds. Five tabs appear on the right: WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, Blog, SEO.
5. Click on each tab to read the copy. Click "Salin" (Copy) to copy it to your clipboard.
6. Paste into your actual WhatsApp broadcast, Instagram post, email tool, blog editor, or website.

## Preset Campaigns
- **Back to School (Januari)** — New school year uniform push
- **Pertengahan Tahun (Jun)** — Mid-year term start
- **Akhir Tahun (Clearance)** — Year-end stock clearance

## Tips
- The more specific your brief, the better the copy. Include actual prices, product names, and your store location.
- Try different tones on the same brief — BM Santai works best for WhatsApp, Formal BM for email.
- You can regenerate one channel without regenerating all five.

## About the API Key
Your Gemini API key is stored in your browser session only. It is never saved to any server. Each generation costs approximately RM0.01–0.05 in API fees billed to your own Google account.
