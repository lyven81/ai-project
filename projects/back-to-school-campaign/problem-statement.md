## The Problem

The owner of a small children's clothing store in Bangi sells school uniforms — white shirts, pinafores, PE attire, school shoes, socks, and bags. Every December/January and June, she needs to push a back-to-school campaign across WhatsApp, Instagram, email, her blog, and Google search. She has no marketing staff — she writes the copy herself, usually one generic paragraph that gets pasted everywhere or posted only on one channel.

The result is weak reach and wasted effort. A WhatsApp broadcast that reads like an Instagram caption gets ignored. An Instagram post with no hook gets 3 likes. Channels that need different voices — urgency for WhatsApp, storytelling for blog, keywords for Google — all get the same flat copy. Free LLM chat tools can generate copy, but she has to re-explain her store, tone, and products every session, and gets one draft back instead of five channel-ready versions.

## Who It Is For

- Owner of a children's clothing store in Bangi, Malaysia
- 1–3 staff, physical shop + WhatsApp catalogue
- RM15K–80K/month revenue
- Sells school uniforms (shirts, pinafores, PE attire, shoes, socks, bags) + casual kids' wear
- Runs marketing personally — WhatsApp broadcast, occasional Instagram, no email list, no blog, no SEO
- Needs back-to-school campaign copy every December/January and June

## Market Fit Verdict

**Upgrades existing**

Free LLM chat produces one draft at a time with no business memory. This app bakes in the store context once and outputs a coordinated 5-channel campaign (WhatsApp, Instagram, email, blog, SEO) in one brief — zero subscription cost via BYO-key.
