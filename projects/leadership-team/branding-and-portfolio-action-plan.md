# Jaya Realty — Branding & Listing Action Plan (sample)

> Fictional sample for the Leadership Team demo. Shows how an independent property agent uses the weekly boardroom to drive branding, listings, and lead generation in parallel.

---

## Positioning

**Who we serve:** Klang Valley upgraders — young families moving from a first condo to a landed home in PJ / Subang / Shah Alam.

**Why us:** Honest pricing, fast photo-to-listing turnaround, and an owner-operator who actually picks up the phone on weekends.

**Tagline:** *Your next home, without the runaround.*

---

## Q2 Priorities (next 12 weeks)

| # | Priority | Owner | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 3 closed deals per month | Amir + Jason Lim (Marketing) | 9 closings by end of Q2 |

---

## Listing Workflow (standardised)

1. **New mandate signed** → listing reference added to `company-state.md`
2. **Anisa** writes listing copy + social caption within 24 hours
3. **Faizal** pushes listing to PropertyGuru, iProperty, Facebook Marketplace, and agent's WhatsApp broadcast
4. **Jason** adds buyers from warm pipeline to a targeted outreach list
5. **Rajesh** pulls 3 recent comparables for the pricing sheet
6. **Siti** schedules viewings and sends reminders
7. **Amir** attends viewings, negotiates, closes

---

## Lead Generation Channels

- **Referrals (primary):** Existing client + family referrals — currently 60% of leads
- **Weekend open houses:** 2–3 per month, targeting high-traffic areas
- **Social media:** Instagram reels + TikTok walkthroughs — growing channel
- **Property portals:** PropertyGuru, iProperty — baseline presence
- **Paid ads:** Facebook lead ads for high-ticket listings only (>RM 1m)

---

## Weekly Boardroom Agenda (standing items)

1. Last week's decisions — status review
2. Rajesh — market movement + comparable sales
3. Jason — lead pipeline + conversion
4. Anisa — content + listing copy progress
5. Faizal — automation + listing distribution status
6. Amir — this week's focus + blockers
7. Mei Ling — strategic observations (positioning, risk, focus)
8. Action items + owners
