🔮 Try Live Demo

📋 Project Overview & Problem Statement

Challenge: Many people seek clarity on personal decisions, relationships, and life transitions but lack a private, non-judgmental space to explore their thoughts. Traditional tarot apps deliver generic card definitions disconnected from the user's actual situation.

Solution: Tarot Guru is a personal AI tarot reading app that runs locally on your computer. You describe your real situation, and the app draws cards and generates a narrative reading that speaks directly to your specific question — not a generic card definition. It uses Claude Opus AI to interpret each card in the context of what you actually asked.

Key Benefits

  • Context-Aware Readings: Every reading is written specifically for your question, not a generic card definition
  • Intelligent Spread Selection: The Guru recommends the right number of cards (1, 3, or Celtic Cross) based on your question complexity
  • Narrative Interpretation: Full story-style reading connecting all cards into one coherent insight
  • Follow-up Chat: Ask follow-up questions in context — the Guru remembers your entire session
  • Session Memory: Every reading is saved locally as JSON — review past sessions anytime
  • Privacy First: Runs entirely on your device; only the question text reaches the Claude API

🖥️ Application Features

🎴 Three Spread Types

Single Card: One card, one focused message — quick check-in or daily energy.
Three-Card (Past/Present/Future): Most versatile spread for everyday questions.
Celtic Cross (10 cards): Full coverage for complex life decisions.

🧠 AI-Recommended Spread

The Guru reads your question and recommends the most appropriate spread type and explains why. You can accept the recommendation or override with your preferred spread — you're always in control.

💬 Follow-Up Chat

After the reading, a chat box lets you ask about specific cards, probe deeper into any theme, or ask for practical next steps. The Guru maintains full session context throughout the conversation.

📁 Session Archive

Every completed reading is automatically saved as a timestamped JSON file on your computer. Includes your question, the cards drawn (upright/reversed), the full reading text, and your chat history.

How a Session Works

  • Step 1: Describe your situation in the text box — the more specific, the more useful the reading
  • Step 2: The Guru recommends a spread type and explains why it fits your question
  • Step 3: Confirm or choose a different spread (1 Card / 3 Cards / Celtic Cross)
  • Step 4: Cards reveal face-up one by one with name, orientation, and active keywords
  • Step 5: A full narrative reading appears, written specifically for your question
  • Step 6: Ask as many follow-up questions as you like — the Guru stays in context

🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

Node.js Express Claude Opus API Vanilla JS ESM Modules dotenv Local JSON Storage

Tarot Guru runs as a lightweight Node.js + Express server on your local machine. The card deck and spread logic are handled entirely in JavaScript. The only external call is to the Claude Opus API, which receives your question and the drawn card names to generate the narrative reading. All session data is stored as JSON files on your device — nothing is persisted remotely.

💡 Design Principles

  • Reflection, not prediction: Tarot Guru explicitly frames every reading as a mirror — it surfaces patterns and blind spots, not predictions. The app's system prompt is designed around this philosophy.
  • Context over definitions: The AI is instructed to connect every card directly to the user's stated situation. A reversed card in a career reading reads very differently from the same card in a relationship reading.
  • User control: The spread recommendation is always optional. The user can always switch to any spread type at will.
  • Local-first privacy: The app is designed to run entirely on the user's device. The only data that leaves is the question text sent to Claude's API.
