Project Overview & Problem Statement

Challenge: A children's clothing store owner in Bangi needs to push back-to-school uniform campaigns every January and June across WhatsApp, Instagram, email, blog, and Google search. She has no marketing staff — she writes one generic paragraph and pastes it everywhere, or skips channels entirely because writing different copy for each platform takes too long.

Solution: Back-to-School gives her a team of 5 AI copywriters, each specialising in one platform's voice. She fills in one campaign brief, picks a tone (BM Santai, Formal, or Campur), and gets five ready-to-paste outputs in Bahasa Melayu — all in under 10 seconds.

Key Benefits

  • One brief, five channels — WhatsApp broadcast, Instagram caption, 3-email drip sequence, blog story, SEO paragraph
  • All in Bahasa Melayu — written for Malaysian parents, not translated from English templates
  • 3 preset campaigns — Back to School (Jan), Mid-Year (Jun), Year-End Clearance — try instantly without typing
  • Less than 1 sen per run — BYO Gemini key, no subscription, no backend costs
  • Copy-paste ready — one-click Salin button per channel, paste straight into the actual platform

The 5 AI Copywriters

Each writer owns one platform and writes in that platform's native voice. Same campaign message, five different tones.

💬

Penulis WhatsApp

Short, urgent broadcast — 4-6 lines max. Ends with a clear CTA like "Balas 1 untuk tempah." Written like a personal message from the shop owner.

📸

Penulis Instagram

Hook in the first line, 3-5 short paragraphs, ends with 5-8 BM hashtags. Every line earns attention for scrolling parents.

📧

Penulis Email

3-email drip sequence: Teaser (spark interest), Tawaran (full offer with prices), Last Call (urgency + closing date). Each with [Nama] personalization.

📝

Penulis Blog

300-500 word storytelling piece. Opens with a relatable parent scenario, weaves in product details naturally, ends with a soft CTA.

🔍

Penulis SEO

150-200 word Google-optimized paragraph targeting "baju sekolah murah Bangi" and similar BM keywords. Includes a meta description.

Application Features

Campaign Brief Form

Fill in store name, location, products and prices, campaign details, and target audience. The form provides the context all 5 writers need.

3 Preset Campaigns

Back to School (Januari), Pertengahan Tahun (Jun), and Akhir Tahun (Clearance) — pre-filled with Kiddoz Uniform House in Bangi as the demo business.

Tone Selector

Choose between BM Santai (casual, like texting a friend), BM Formal (professional), or Campur (BM-English mix). Each tone changes how all 5 writers speak.

Copy-to-Clipboard

One-click "Salin" button per channel. Copy the WhatsApp broadcast and paste it straight into your broadcast list. No reformatting needed.

Per-Channel Regeneration

Don't like the Instagram caption but love the WhatsApp version? Regenerate one channel independently without rerunning the whole campaign.

System Architecture

  • Single self-contained HTML file — no build tools, no dependencies
  • Campaign brief collected via form, sent as structured context in Gemini system prompt
  • 5 specialist writer personas defined in system prompt with per-channel format rules
  • Gemini returns structured JSON with 5 keys (whatsapp, instagram, email, blog, seo)
  • API key stored in sessionStorage — never persisted, never sent anywhere except Google
  • Per-channel regeneration uses a separate prompt targeting only that writer

Development Setup

Prerequisites

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- A Gemini API key (free at aistudio.google.com/apikey)

Quick Start

1. Open demo.html in your browser
2. Paste your Gemini API key and click Activate
3. Click a preset campaign or fill in your own brief
4. Click "Jana Kempen" — 5 tabs appear with BM copy
5. Click "Salin" to copy, paste into your actual platform
